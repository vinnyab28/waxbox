Client Policies

Thoughtfully designed to protect your time — and mine — so every appointment can be the experience you deserve.

A Note from Mayela

These policies come from a place of care — for you and for every client who walks through my door. When we respect each other's time, I can give every appointment the full attention and quality it deserves. Thank you for taking a moment to read through them.

— Late Arrivals

  • I hold a 6-minute grace period to give you time to settle in.
  • If you arrive more than 6 minutes late, your service may need to be adjusted to fit the remaining time — so we can still deliver the quality you came for.
  • My priority is always to send you home happy.

— Late Cancellations & No-Shows

  • Everyone gets a one-time grace — life happens, and I understand that.
  • After a second late cancellation, a minimum $25 deposit (or more depending on the service) will be required to secure future bookings.
  • Deposits are applied directly toward your appointment — they're yours.
  • Deposits are non-refundable if the appointment is cancelled late or missed, as that time was held exclusively for you.
  • No-show appointments are subject to a charge of 50% of the scheduled service cost.

— Cancellations & Rescheduling

  • Please give at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule.
  • This allows me to offer that time to another client who may be waiting — and keeps everything running smoothly for everyone.

— My Commitment to You

  • These policies exist to protect the quality of care I can offer every single client.
  • When your time and my time are respected, I can bring my full focus, skill, and warmth to every appointment.
  • You deserve that — and I'm committed to delivering it, every time.

By booking an appointment with The Wax Box by MC, you acknowledge and agree to these policies. If you have any questions, I'm always happy to chat — reach me at the.wax.box@outlook.com.
